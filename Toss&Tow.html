Toss N' Tow

Toss N' Tow is designed to transform the chore of hauling gear for your beach days and tailgates into an opportunity for convenient, all-terrain fun. Once you've found your spot, this rugged, wheeled carrier can unlock and separate to become a pair of mobile cornhole boards. At the end of the day, simply lock the boards back together, repack your things into the storage chamber, and leave.

This project was completed in collaboration with Veronica Cargay, Lui Hu, Maitlyn Lang, and Aditi Vardhan.
